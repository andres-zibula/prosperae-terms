Prosperae – Account Deletion

Last updated: January 14, 2026

This page explains how to delete your Prosperae account and what happens to your data when you do so.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Prosperae app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the Settings screen.
  4. Tap Delete account.
  5. Confirm the deletion request.

What data is deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is deleted:

  • Authentication record (Firebase Authentication), including your user identifier and sign-in provider association.
  • Feedback/support messages you submitted from within the app (stored in Firestore), including message content and timestamps.
  • Subscription entitlement association managed via RevenueCat (store purchase history remains managed by Google Play / Apple).
  • All local financial data stored on your device, including transactions, accounts, budgets, currencies, and country settings.

Data retention

After account deletion is confirmed, we delete the cloud-stored data described above and remove all local data associated with the app from your device. We do not retain personal data after account deletion, except where required by law.

Need help?

If you cannot access the app or need assistance with account deletion, contact us at prosperaeapp@gmail.com and include the email address you use to sign in.
