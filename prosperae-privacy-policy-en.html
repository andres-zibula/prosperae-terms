Prosperae – Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 12, 2026

Summary (non-binding): Prosperae is a personal finance app. Your financial data is stored locally on your device. We use third-party services for authentication, subscriptions, analytics, and error reporting. Analytics and crash reporting are optional and can be disabled in the App’s Privacy settings.

1. Who we are

Prosperae is developed and operated by Andres Zibula, located in Uruguay (“we”, “us”).

2. Information we collect

  • Account information: name, email address, profile photo URL, and a user identifier provided by Google or Apple.
  • Local financial data: all financial information you enter is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Usage analytics (optional): screen views, session events, basic interactions, and subscription-related events.
  • Diagnostics (optional): crash logs and performance data such as stack traces, app version, OS version, and device model.
  • Feedback: message content, timestamp, and account identifiers when you submit feedback.

3. How we use your information

  • To authenticate users and manage accounts.
  • To provide and maintain core app functionality.
  • To manage subscriptions and entitlements.
  • To respond to feedback and support requests.
  • To improve stability and performance.

4. Third-party services

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Analytics (optional)
  • Firestore (feedback only)
  • Sentry (optional diagnostics)
  • RevenueCat (subscriptions)

    • Authentication: Users authenticate using Google on Android and Sign in with Apple on iOS.

5. Data security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

All data transmitted to third-party services is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols.

6. Tracking and advertising

We do not use data for tracking purposes as defined by Apple, and we do not link user data with third-party data for advertising.

7. Your choices

  • You can disable analytics and diagnostics in the App’s Privacy settings.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated cloud data from within the App.
  • You can manage subscriptions through your app store account.

8. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. The effective date will be updated accordingly.

10. Contact

Contact us at prosperaeapp@gmail.com.
